## Introduction

Every successful packaging design project follows a structured process that balances creativity with strategy, aesthetics with functionality. Today, I'm pulling back the curtain on my six-phase packaging design process.

## Phase 1: Discovery & Research (Week 1)

The foundation of great design is understanding. Before touching any design tools, I invest significant time in:

**Client interviews** to understand:
- Business goals and KPIs
- Target audience and personas
- Brand positioning and values
- Budget and timeline constraints

**Market research** including:
- Competitor analysis
- Retail environment study
- Consumer behavior insights
- Material and printing capabilities

**Output:** Creative brief, mood boards, research document
