## Why 3D Mockups Matter

High-quality 3D mockups have become essential in modern packaging design workflows. They serve multiple purposes:

- **Client presentations:** Help stakeholders visualize the final product
- **Marketing materials:** Create assets before physical production
- **E-commerce:** Generate product images for online stores
- **Investment pitches:** Demonstrate product vision to investors
- **Social media:** Share work without revealing client confidential information
