## The Power of Color in Packaging

Color is one of the most powerful tools in a package designer's arsenal. Research shows that up to 90% of snap judgments about products are based on color alone. Understanding color psychology is essential for creating packaging that resonates with your target audience and stands out on crowded shelves.
