REQUIRED IMAGES TO ADD
======================

To remove the image errors, you need to add these placeholder images:

PRIORITY 1 - Profile Images (Add These First):
----------------------------------------------
/public/avatar.jpg              - Your profile photo (400x400px)
/public/hero-image.jpg          - Homepage hero image (1920x1080px)
/public/logo.png                - Your logo
/public/favicon.ico             - Browser icon
/public/og-image.jpg            - Social sharing image (1200x630px)
/public/about-profile.jpg       - About page photo (800x800px)

QUICK FIX - Use Placeholder Images:
-----------------------------------
Option 1: Download from Unsplash.com (free)
   - Search: "packaging design", "designer portrait", "hero background"
   - Download and rename to match the filenames above

Option 2: Use stock photos you already have
   - Just rename them to match the required filenames

Option 3: Upload through Admin Panel
   - Go to: http://localhost:3000/admin
   - Edit your profile and upload your actual photos
   - For projects: Edit each project and upload real images

The errors will disappear once you add at least the Priority 1 images!
